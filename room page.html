Welcome! Please choose the room that suits your preference and budget. Enjoy your vacation.

Standard Room




Enjoy the comfort and beauty of nature in our Standard Room at LuxeLafe Glampsite, which presents a simple yet pampering stay with adequate facilities to make your vacation moments special.




  • Basic amenities and standard comforts.
  • Equipped with a bed, desk and private bathroom.
  • The space is functional with a simple design.
  • Cleaning service and linen change.

Deluxe Room




With an elegant design and a touch of luxury, our Deluxe Rooms at LuxeLeaf Glampsite bring a special feel to your stay. With more complete facilities, this place is suitable for those who want comfort and luxury in the midst of natural beauty.




  • Offers more facilities than Standard Room.
  • More elegant interior design and higher quality furnishings.
  • Comes with additional amenities such as a seating area, refrigerator, or private balcony.
  • Additional services such as 24-hour room service or access to more exclusive public facilities.

Executive Suite




Explore true luxury at LuxeLeaf Glampsite with a stay in our Executive Suite. Spacious spaces and exclusive facilities create a special atmosphere, providing an extraordinary and unforgettable stay amidst the mesmerizing charm of nature.




  • Larger rooms with separate sleeping and living areas.
  • Additional amenities such as kitchenette, minibar, or bathtub.
  • A higher level of personalized service, such as concierge services or private tour guides.
  • Comes with exclusive access to glamping facilities, such as a private lounge or private pool.
