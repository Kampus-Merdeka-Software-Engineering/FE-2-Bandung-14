LUXELEAF GLAMPSITE

LuxeLeaf Glampsite is located on a hillside that spoils visitors with spectacular views of the beachs and verdant forests. Each of our glamping tents is elegantly designed and equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and luxurious stay. Our tents provide an authentic natural setting while still offering comforts such as custom-designed beds, luxurious furnishings, and spa-style toiletries.


Guests can savour their own intimacy in the silence of nature while enjoying world-class amenities. LuxeLeaf Glampsite has a cosy lounge area, a gathering place to enjoy a cup of coffee or wine in the evening. For the adventurous, there are various nature activities such as hiking, camping, and other outdoor activities.
